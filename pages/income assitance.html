Providing financial support to low-income families across Gujarat

About the Yojna

The Income Assistance Yojna is a government initiative aimed at providing financial assistance to eligible citizens from low-income households. The assistance helps to uplift the standard of living for the underprivileged members of society.

Eligibility Criteria

Resident of Gujarat

Applicants must be a resident of Gujarat to apply for this scheme. Proof of residence may be required.

Income Level

Your annual income should be below the prescribed threshold for eligibility. Please refer to the income guidelines.

Age Group

Applicants must be between the age group of 18-60 years to qualify for the scheme.

Documents Required

Aadhar Card

Aadhar Card is a mandatory identification proof required for verification and records.

Income Certificate

Income Certificate is needed to verify your family's income for eligibility criteria.

Proof of Residence

Proof of Residence verifies your current address, such as a utility bill or lease agreement.

Age Proof

Age Proof could be your birth certificate or any official document verifying your date of birth.

Bank Account Details

Bank Account Details include your account number and bank branch information for financial transactions.

How to Apply

Step 1

Visit your nearest government office to collect the application form.

Step 2

Fill in the application form with all the necessary details and documents.

Step 3

Submit the application form at the office for further processing.
