Eligibility & Required Documents

Eligibility Criteria
  • Must be a citizen of India
  • Age 60 or above
  • Income less than ₹30,000/month
  • Residing in India
Required Documents
  • Age Proof (Aadhar Card, Birth Certificate)
  • Identity Proof (Aadhar Card, Passport)
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Account Details

About the Scheme

This scheme provides financial support to senior citizens above 60 years of age who have limited income and no other means of support. The scheme aims to help improve their quality of life.

  • Monthly Pension Support
  • Regular Payment Disbursements
  • Easy Application Process
