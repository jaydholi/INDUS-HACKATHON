General Overview

The platform serves as a bridge between citizens and government welfare schemes. By accessing this platform, you acknowledge that:

  • You are responsible for maintaining the accuracy of your information.
  • The platform is a facilitator and does not directly manage the approval of applications.
  • Notifications provided are intended for guidance and convenience.

The platform supports the following services:

  • Access to all active welfare schemes in Ahmedabad.
  • Detailed information about eligibility criteria and required documents.
  • Real-time tracking of applications.
  • Timely updates and notifications about application status.

User Obligations

All users must adhere to the following rules while using the platform:

  • Provide truthful, accurate, and complete information during registration and application processes.
  • Safeguard your login credentials to prevent unauthorized access to your account.
  • Avoid any form of misconduct, such as submitting fake documents or providing false information.
  • Refrain from activities that may disrupt the platform's services or infrastructure.

Violation of these obligations may result in the suspension or termination of your account, as well as potential legal action.

Data Privacy and Security

We prioritize the protection of your personal data. By using this platform, you agree to the collection and usage of your data as outlined in our Privacy Policy. Key points include:

  • Your data will not be shared with unauthorized third parties.
  • The platform is regularly updated with security measures to prevent breaches.

Scheme Application Rules

When applying for schemes, users must adhere to these guidelines:

  • Ensure that all documents are valid, up-to-date, and meet the scheme's requirements.
  • Submit applications within the specified timeframes to avoid disqualification.
  • Be aware that application approval is subject to government verification and discretion.

Communication and Notifications

The platform provides notifications to keep you updated about important developments, including:
