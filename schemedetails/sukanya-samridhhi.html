Eligibility Criteria

The PM Ujjwala Yojana is designed to provide free LPG connections to women from economically weaker sections to promote clean cooking fuel.

Women Beneficiaries

Only adult women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) families are eligible to apply for a free LPG connection under this scheme.

Valid Identification

Applicants must provide valid **Aadhaar Card**, **Ration Card**, and **proof of residence** to avail of the benefits.
