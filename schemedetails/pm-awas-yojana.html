Eligibility Criteria

The Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana is designed to provide financial security for the future of girl children. Below are the eligibility criteria for enrolling in the scheme.

Girl Child Beneficiaries

Only a **girl child** can be enrolled under this scheme. The girl must be below the age of 10 years at the time of account opening.

Valid Identity Proof

Applicants must provide valid **Aadhaar Card** and **birth certificate of the girl child** to confirm eligibility.

Parent/Guardian Criteria

The account must be opened by the girl child's **parents or legal guardian**. Only one account is allowed per girl child.

Residency

The scheme is available to all **Indian citizens**, and the account can be opened at any authorized bank or post office.

Benefits of the Scheme

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) is a government initiative aimed at providing affordable housing to economically weaker sections, ensuring a safe and secure home for every family.

Affordable Housing

The scheme provides **subsidized housing** for **Economically Weaker Sections (EWS), Low Income Groups (LIG), and Middle Income Groups (MIG)**.

Interest Subsidy

Eligible applicants receive **interest subsidies** under the **Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS)**, reducing home loan burdens.

Eligibility for All

Both urban and rural applicants, including **women, senior citizens, and differently-abled individuals**, can benefit from this scheme.

Slum Rehabilitation

Under PMAY, **slum dwellers are provided with better housing** through in-situ development programs.

Required Documents

Ensure you have the following documents ready before applying for the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana:

Aadhaar Card

Aadhaar card is mandatory for identity verification and linking with the PMAY application.

Address Proof

Documents like a ration card, voter ID, utility bill, or Aadhaar card to verify residence.

Income Certificate

A valid income certificate is required to determine eligibility under the **EWS, LIG, or MIG categories**.

Property Documents

Documents like property registration papers or an affidavit declaring **no existing pucca house**.

Bank Account Details

An active **bank account in the applicant’s name** is required for financial transactions and subsidy credits.

Mobile Number

Registered mobile number for receiving updates, alerts, and OTP verification during the application process.
