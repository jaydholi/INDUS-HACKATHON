Ayushman Bharat Scheme

The world’s largest health insurance scheme, empowering citizens and improving access to healthcare services.

Join the initiative to ensure better health for every citizen in India. The Ayushman Bharat Scheme is a milestone towards Universal Health Coverage (UHC).

Eligibility Criteria

This scheme targets millions of families across India who are underprivileged and lack access to quality healthcare services.

Families Below Poverty Line

Eligible families are those who fall under the Below Poverty Line (BPL) category. This ensures the poorest communities are supported first.

Low Income Families

Incomes that are insufficient to afford private healthcare but still above the poverty line are also considered under the scheme.

Rural and Urban Coverage

Both rural and urban populations are eligible, making this a nationwide initiative that includes all parts of the country.

Benefits of the Scheme

Ayushman Bharat offers a wide range of benefits to eligible citizens, ensuring better health care access and improved medical facilities.

Cashless Treatment

Receive treatment at empaneled hospitals without paying any money upfront. The costs are covered under the scheme.

Comprehensive Coverage

Extensive coverage that includes surgeries, treatments, and diagnostics. The scheme covers hospitalization, surgeries, and even pre-existing conditions.

Free Health Checkups

Eligibility for free health checkups under the program ensures regular health monitoring for individuals and families.

Over 10,000 Hospitals Empaneled

Hospitals across India are a part of the Ayushman Bharat network, ensuring better accessibility to healthcare services nationwide.

Required Documents

Ensure you have the following documents ready before applying for the scheme:

Aadhaar Card

A government-issued Aadhaar card for identity verification. This is essential for verification purposes.

Income Proof

Documents such as salary slips, income certificates, or tax returns to confirm the applicant's income level.

Address Proof

Documents like utility bills, ration card, or voter ID to confirm the applicant's address within India.
