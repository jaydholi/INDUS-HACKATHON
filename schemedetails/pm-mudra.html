Eligibility Criteria

The PM Mudra Yojana is designed to provide financial assistance to micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs) and individuals seeking to start or expand their businesses.

Entrepreneurs

Individuals who want to start a new business or expand an existing business are eligible for financial assistance under the scheme.

Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs)

Existing micro, small, and medium enterprises are eligible to apply for loans under the PM Mudra Yojana to meet working capital or business expansion needs.

Business Bank Account

Applicants must have an **active business bank account** for the loan disbursement under the scheme.

Exclusions

Individuals and entities involved in agricultural activities, or those who already possess substantial assets, are **not eligible** for the scheme.

Benefits of the Scheme

PM Mudra Yojana provides financial assistance to small and micro enterprises, helping to promote entrepreneurship and business growth.

Easy Loan Access

Under the scheme, loans up to ₹10 lakh are provided to micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs) to support business activities.

Support for Entrepreneurs

The scheme primarily benefits individuals, startups, and small businesses, helping them grow and expand their ventures.

Boost to MSMEs

PM Mudra Yojana encourages the development of micro, small, and medium enterprises, contributing to job creation and economic growth.

Flexible Loan Terms

Loans under the scheme come with flexible repayment terms, ensuring easier accessibility and financial relief to borrowers.

Required Documents

Ensure you have the following documents ready before applying for the PM Mudra Yojana:

Aadhaar Card

Aadhaar card is mandatory for identity verification and linking with the PM Mudra Yojana system.

Business Proof

Documents proving the existence of your business, such as GST registration, partnership deed, or certificate of incorporation.

Bank Account Details

Active bank account details, including **IFSC code**, for receiving the loan amount directly under the PM Mudra Yojana.

Business Plan or Project Report

A detailed business plan or project report to outline how the loan will be utilized and the projected outcomes.

Mobile Number

Registered mobile number for receiving updates, alerts, and OTP verification during the application process.
