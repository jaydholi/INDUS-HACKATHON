PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana

A government initiative providing **financial assistance of ₹6,000 per year** to small and marginal farmers.

Eligibility Criteria

The PM Kisan Yojana is designed to provide financial support to eligible farmers across India.

Small and Marginal Farmers

Farmers owning **cultivable land up to 2 hectares** are eligible to receive financial benefits under the scheme.

Land Ownership

Applicants must have **official land records** showing ownership of agricultural land.

Bank Account Linked with Aadhaar

Farmers must have an **active bank account linked to their Aadhaar card** for Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).

Exclusions

Institutional landholders, government employees, and taxpayers are **not eligible** for the scheme.

Benefits of the Scheme

PM Kisan Yojana provides financial assistance to small and marginal farmers, ensuring their economic stability and agricultural growth.

Direct Income Support

Eligible farmers receive ₹6,000 per year directly in their bank accounts in three equal installments.

Support for Small & Marginal Farmers

The scheme primarily benefits small and marginal farmers, helping them meet agricultural expenses and improve productivity.

Nationwide Coverage

PM Kisan benefits millions of farmers across India, ensuring financial aid reaches those in need.

Seamless Fund Transfer

Payments are transferred through **Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)**, ensuring transparency and efficiency.

Required Documents

Ensure you have the following documents ready before applying for the PM Kisan Yojana:

Aadhaar Card

Aadhaar card is mandatory for farmer verification and linking with the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) system.

Land Ownership Documents

Land records such as **Khasra-Khatauni**, land deeds, or revenue records proving ownership of cultivable land.

Bank Account Details

Active bank account details, including **IFSC code**, to receive the scheme's financial assistance directly.

Mobile Number

Registered mobile number for receiving scheme updates and OTP verification during application.
